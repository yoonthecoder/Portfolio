GRAPHIC DESIGN

Chibi's Adventure
Package Design

Project Overview

This project is to create a brand identity for a fictional board game company, “Chibi’s adventure” created by the in-class client, and to design a logo, business card based on the client’s needs by engaging with a client in the design process.

Timeline May 4, 2020 – June 30, 2020
Role Graphic Desginer
Tools Adobe Photoshop
01

Client Meeting

First, I had a meeting with an assigned in-class client to better understand the client’s needs. First, I did some research about some competitors and tried to define the target audience/demographics and goals of the projects. Also, the client confirmed the main color palette she would like to go with and the overall theme of the design.

02

Logo Design

Based on the client’s needs and requests understood in the previous meeting, I brainstormed the logo ideas and sketched five different versions of logos on the paper considering the colour theme we decided. After colouring them, I presented them to the client to let her choose the best one.

03

Business Card Design

Using the final logo created in the previous stage, I created a business card which best represents the company’s values and give customers a good impression.

04

Package Design Mock-ups

In this stage, I created an original packaging art for a boxed product. Since my assigned company is a D&D style board game company, I created 3 graphics for a board game box which include 1 front/top and 2 sides of the box. The graphics are created for print.
