What The Craft Website

Project Overview

The purpose of this case study is to analyze the website I see often (freecodecamp.org), look for patterns and problems with the design, identify ideal users, then create mockups and conduct usability tests.

Timeline Jan 2021 - Feb 2021
Role Front-end Web Developer
Tools HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap
01

Assess

This stage was for assessing and understanding some challenges and pain points users might experience. After having used the website for several months, I was able to find some issues I would like to fix and improve.

02

Empathize

To better understand users, I defined 3 main user archetypes and set one of them as a major persona. Based on the persona, I created a journey map to understand the whole experience from the user’s perspective by visualizing the process she goes through. It helped me to get a sense of the user’s motivations, needs or some pain points and also discover some opportunities to focus on going forward.

03

Sketch

Before going through the actual design work, I brainstormed all the ideas I gathered from the previous stages and started with some free-sketching of the key pages based on some challenges and the task flows which are covered earlier.

04

Design

Design high-fidelity prototypes and a style tile with the redesigned UI components.

05

Evaluate

After creating the high-fidelity prototypes, I tested the usability using Invision. I sent out the link to my peers and saw how the interactions flowed in real time, and was able to get some revision ideas.

06

Final Revision

Lastly, I went through the final revision process based on the feedback I got and some issues pointed out from my peers.
